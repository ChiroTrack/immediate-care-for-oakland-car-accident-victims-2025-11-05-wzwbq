About Oakland Immediate Care

Oakland Immediate Care was founded in 2015 by a group of experienced healthcare professionals who recognized a critical gap in post-accident care services. After witnessing countless car accident victims struggle to find coordinated, immediate medical attention, our founders established a facility specifically designed to meet the urgent needs of injured patients. Starting with a small team of dedicated chiropractors and physical therapists, we've grown into a comprehensive medical center with a mission to revolutionize how Oakland residents receive emergency care after vehicular accidents. Our commitment to excellence and patient-centered care has made us the trusted choice for thousands of families during their most difficult moments.

Today, Oakland Immediate Care operates on core values of compassion, expertise, and efficiency. We believe that every accident victim deserves immediate access to world-class medical care without the bureaucratic delays of traditional emergency rooms. Our multidisciplinary team works collaboratively to provide personalized treatment plans that address not just the obvious injuries, but the underlying complications that often emerge days or weeks after an accident. We're committed to using the latest diagnostic and treatment technologies, maintaining the highest standards of professional certification, and continuously educating our team to deliver the best possible outcomes for our patients. Whether you're suffering from whiplash, back injuries, joint trauma, or complex multi-system injuries, Oakland Immediate Care is here to support your complete recovery journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and treatment options

How quickly can I get an appointment after my car accident?

We prioritize car accident victims and offer same-day appointments whenever possible. Most patients can be seen within 2-4 hours of calling. If you arrive at our facility without an appointment, we have a dedicated urgent care area that can accommodate walk-ins immediately. We understand that time is critical after an accident, so our entire system is designed to minimize waiting and get you into treatment as quickly as possible. For the fastest service, call us directly at the number provided, and our scheduling team will find you the next available slot.

Do you accept insurance, or do I need to pay out of pocket?

We accept most major insurance plans, including auto insurance, health insurance, and workers' compensation. Our billing team works directly with your insurance company to handle claims and maximize your coverage. If you don't have insurance or your plan has limitations, we offer flexible payment plans to ensure cost doesn't prevent you from getting the care you need. During your first appointment, we'll verify your coverage and explain all your options. We're committed to making quality care accessible regardless of your financial situation.

What types of injuries do you treat?

We treat the full spectrum of car accident injuries, including whiplash, neck and back injuries, joint trauma, soft tissue damage, concussions, and complex multi-system injuries. Our multidisciplinary team can handle everything from minor strains to serious trauma. We also specialize in identifying hidden injuries that don't appear immediately after an accident but can develop into serious problems if left untreated. Our specialists have extensive experience with motor vehicle accident cases and understand the unique patterns of injury that result from different types of collisions.

How long will my recovery take?

Recovery time varies significantly depending on the severity of your injuries. Minor soft tissue injuries may resolve in 2-4 weeks with proper treatment, while more serious injuries could require 2-6 months or longer. The good news is that our coordinated, multidisciplinary approach typically reduces recovery time compared to traditional treatment methods. During your initial evaluation, our doctors will assess your specific injuries and provide a realistic timeline for your recovery. We'll track your progress closely and adjust your treatment plan as needed to keep you on the fastest path to full recovery.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any medical records from the accident (police reports, emergency room documents, imaging results). If you have photos of the accident scene or vehicle damage, those can be helpful for our records. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the areas being examined. If you've been to other providers since the accident, bring those records as well. Our intake team will guide you through the process and ask any additional questions we need.
